About Us

Who We Are ?

Sri Surya Education Economic and social Development charitable trust is service minded voluntary & non-governmental organization was established in 2005 and under the Tamilnadu trust registration act 1956 having registered office at Gingee Taluk of Villupuram District ,Tamil Nadu. It began its activities by organizing need based welfare programmes, women empowerment through SHGs and community development programmes.

Background History

Sri Suriya Education, Economic and social charitable trust is a Non-Govt , not a profit organization, has been operating under the able guidance of J.Sumathy , Managing Trustee with the aim of protect and promote the rights of marginalized whose life is hard, precarious. We began our work initially at Ottampattu now it has been expanded into 40 Villages across Gingee Block and Melmalaiyanur Block and Vikkravandi, Kanai ,VallamBlock.

Our Mission

Activities


Sri Suriya Nursery and Primary School

At Sri Suriya Nursery and Primary School, we believe that every child has the potential to excel and make a positive impact on the world. Our nurturing environment, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methods create a foundation for lifelong learning and success. Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity in students. We strive to provide a well-rounded education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also emphasizes the development of essential life skills and character traits such as resilience, empathy, and leadership.


WASH Awareness

At Sri Suriya, we understand the importance of promoting good WASH practices to ensure the health and well-being of our students, staff, and community. Through our WASH Awareness program, we aim to educate and empower individuals to adopt healthy habits that prevent the spread of disease and improve overall quality of life. Water is essential for life, and access to clean water is a basic human right. Through our WASH program, we emphasize the importance of safe water sources and proper water management to prevent waterborne diseases and ensure the health and safety of our community.


ECO Development (tree plantation)

Trees are essential for maintaining a healthy planet. They provide oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, improve air quality, prevent soil erosion, and provide habitat for wildlife. At Sri Suriya, we recognize the crucial role that trees play in our ecosystem, and we are dedicated to planting and caring for trees to ensure a greener and more sustainable future for generations to come. Through our Eco Development program, we actively engage our students, staff, and community members in tree plantation activities. We believe that by involving everyone in the process, we can instill a sense of responsibility and ownership for our environment and inspire positive change.


Micro Credit Loan Support To Rural Poor Women

At Sri Suriya, we believe in the power of economic empowerment to transform lives and communities. Through our Micro Credit Loan Support initiative, we provide access to small loans to rural women who lack access to traditional banking services and face barriers to economic opportunity. Our program aims to break the cycle of poverty by providing women with the resources they need to start or expand their own businesses, generate income, and support their families. We recognize that women are often the backbone of rural economies, and by investing in their entrepreneurship and leadership, we can create positive ripple effects that benefit entire communities.


Tailoring Centre And Computer Literacy

Welcome to Sri Suriya's Tailoring Centre and Computer Literacy program, where we offer comprehensive training in both the art of tailoring and essential computer skills. Our Tailoring Centre provides courses ranging from basic stitching techniques to advanced garment construction and fashion design, while our Computer Literacy program covers everything from basic computer operation to internet research and programming fundamentals. Whether you're passionate about fashion or aiming to enhance your digital skills, our integrated approach to education ensures a holistic learning experience tailored to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students. Join us and embark on a journey of creativity, exploration, and lifelong learning.
